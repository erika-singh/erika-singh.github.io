001

AI Education Workshop

Explored real-world AI use cases and built a workshop to simplify complex concepts for non-technical teams. This workshop was adopted officially and presented by me to a 20+ person strategic engagement team.

How do LLMs work — Locked Room Analogy

Imagine you're passed a note through a door. You have to write back based only on what you know from reading thousands of books before being locked in. You've never seen anything new since then, and you can't leave to check. That's how an LLM works.

Use Cases — Synthetic Data

Data Accountability: AI can stamp and verify an AI-finished or started a document to confirm it matches a valid format.
Predictive Analytics: For cases with limited data, AI can generate synthetic data to train models without real examples.
Pattern Sensing: Detects when a trigger or engagement metric drops 15% of Q3 from Q1 then alerts.
A/B Testing: AI can create and test multiple campaign versions simultaneously for faster insights.

002

Customer Retention Through Predictive Analytics

Built a predictive model to identify customers who are most likely to return to a local theatre in Kingston, ON. This model enables insights to enable targeted marketing strategies to improve retention and increase revenue.

The Problem

Breaking down the drivers of customer retention to identify where data could create the most impact. This structure guided the development of a predictive model focused on high-return customers and more efficient marketing decisions.

What I Built

A classification model to predict whether a customer was likely to return, using behavioral and demographic data. The model enabled segmentation of customers based on return likelihood, allowing for more targeted and efficient decision-making.

The Outcome

Customer segmentation enabled targeted outreach, improving marketing efficiency and focusing efforts on high-value customers most likely to return and drive revenue growth.

Before Model Deployment

Promotions were sent broadly to all customers, with limited insight into who was likely to return.

After Model Deployment

Customer segmentation enabled targeted outreach, improving marketing efficiency and focusing efforts on high-value customers.
